Tune HIS

Comprehensive hospital management system designed to streamline operations across departments.

  • 200+ integrated modules covering all hospital operations
  • Patient registration, appointments, billing, and insurance processing
  • Multi-location support with customizable workflows

Tune HIS is an end-to-end ERP solution for healthcare facilities, featuring 50+ integrated modules that manage every aspect of hospital operations. From patient registration and appointments to billing, insurance processing, and bed management, the system creates a seamless workflow environment while ensuring compliance with healthcare standards.

With specialized modules for OP/IP management, MRD, insurance handling, and customizable billing policies, Tune HIS adapts to facilities of all sizes across multiple locations. The system's intuitive interface—designed with input from healthcare professionals—reduces training time while improving staff efficiency, ultimately enabling better patient care delivery through harmonious connection of people and processes.

Diagnostic Suite

Integrated laboratory and imaging solution with comprehensive device connectivity.

  • Bi-directional integration with 180+ laboratory instruments
  • Built-in PACS viewer and radiology workflow management
  • Sample tracking, result validation, and quality control

Hostune's Diagnostic Suite combines Laboratory Information System (LIS) and Radiology Information System (RIS) with PACS capabilities to create a complete diagnostic ecosystem. The system manages everything from sample collection and test scheduling to result validation and reporting, with built-in support for over 180 laboratory instruments including Beckman Coulter, Radiometer ABL, and Vitros systems.

The solution features bi-directional machine integration using HL7 and ASTM protocols, barcode generation, test templates, TAT monitoring, and outsourced test management. For radiology, it provides scheduling, worklist management, reporting templates, and a built-in PACS viewer, enabling clinicians to access images and reports directly within their workflow. Quality controls, analytical reports, and trend analysis tools further enhance diagnostic accuracy and operational efficiency.

Clinical Workspace

AI-enhanced EMR system with specialized tools for various clinical scenarios.

  • Dedicated CPOE system with AI support for physicians and nurses
  • ICD 10 & 11 integration with WHO's official database
  • Specialized modules for OT, emergency, dialysis, and physiotherapy

Hostune's Clinical Workspace provides a comprehensive EMR with CPOE capabilities, offering dedicated interfaces for physicians and nurses. The AI-supported system manages patient queues, clinical assessments, allergies, and diagnoses with ICD 10 & 11 integration. Clinical documentation includes rich text formatting, templates, and chronic condition tracking to enhance continuity of care.

Specialized modules address unique requirements for OT management, emergency care, daycare, dialysis, and physiotherapy. Features like drug interaction alerts, investigation ordering, procedure scheduling, and trend analysis of lab results support clinical decision-making. The discharge summary module automates data collection while maintaining compliance with documentation standards, significantly reducing administrative burden for healthcare providers.

Pharmacy Suite

End-to-end medication and inventory management with intelligent forecasting.

  • Multiple store types with FEFO/FIFO protocols
  • Automated purchase requisitions based on consumption patterns
  • ABC & FSN analysis for inventory optimization

Hostune's Pharmacy Suite provides comprehensive medication management with point-of-sale capabilities, prescription tracking, and FEFO/FIFO protocols. The robust inventory component handles multiple store types, item coding, manufacturer data, and drug classification while ensuring regulatory compliance for scheduled medications.

The system's intelligent procurement workflow automates purchase requisitions based on consumption patterns and reorder levels, with approval mechanisms and supplier communications. Advanced features include ABC & FSN analysis, expired item tracking, transfer management between locations, and real-time inventory verification. Integration with billing ensures accurate medication charging while dispensing notifications improve patient medication adherence.

Analytics Platform

Data-driven decision support system with customizable dashboards and reports.

  • Real-time visualization of key performance indicators
  • Drill-down reports and graphical statistics
  • NABH compliance and quality dashboards

The Analytics Platform provides real-time visualization of key performance indicators through customizable dashboards and drill-down reports. The system offers specialized reporting for clinical outcomes, financial performance, inventory management, and quality metrics, supporting NABH compliance and audit requirements. Enterprise Management

Integrated financial, human resources, and administrative solutions for healthcare.

  • GST-compliant financial management with multi-currency support
  • Complete employee lifecycle management with healthcare-specific features
  • Asset tracking and administrative workflow automation

Hostune's Enterprise Management module handles accounts payable/receivable, general ledger, and financial reporting while ensuring GST compliance with appropriate HSN/SAC coding. Integration Engine

Powerful connectivity platform linking clinical devices, external systems, and services.

  • Industry-standard protocols (HL7, ASTM) for seamless data exchange
  • Bi-directional communication with laboratory and clinical equipment
  • Integration with payment gateways, messaging services, and health information exchanges

Hostune's Integration Engine facilitates seamless data exchange between hospital systems and external devices or services through industry-standard protocols like HL7 and ASTM. The platform supports bi-directional communication with laboratory instruments, imaging systems, physiological monitors, and other clinical equipment, eliminating manual data entry and improving accuracy.

Beyond device connectivity, the engine integrates with external services including payment gateways, SMS/WhatsApp messaging, accounting packages, and national health information exchanges. The flexible architecture accommodates both current requirements and future technologies, protecting client investments while enabling continuous innovation. Dedicated data logs and monitoring tools ensure reliable communication and troubleshooting capabilities.
