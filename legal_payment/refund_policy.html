Refund Policy 
At our app, we strive to provide a seamless and convenient online doctor appointment experience. Please review our refund policy below to understand the terms and conditions related to refunds.
Online Payment and Refunds
Refund Eligibility: -
•	Online Payment Option: We offer an online payment option for your convenience.
•	Refund Handling: All refunds for confirmed appointment orders will be handled directly by the hospital.

Refund Process
•	Before Appointment Date: If you need to cancel your appointment and request a refund, please do so before the appointment date. Refunds will be processed according to the hospital's policies.
•	After Appointment Date: Please note that refund requests will not be entertained after the appointment date has passed.
•	Initiating a Refund: To initiate a refund, please contact the hospital directly. Provide them with your appointment details and any other relevant information.
•	Processing Time: The processing time for refunds may vary depending on the hospital's policies and procedures.

For any questions or concerns regarding our refund policy, please contact our customer support.
