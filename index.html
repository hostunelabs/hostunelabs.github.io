Frequently Asked Questions

1. Does Hostune support multi-location healthcare facilities?

Yes, Hostune's solutions are designed for multi-location support, allowing centralized data management and real-time monitoring across all facilities to ensure consistent service delivery.

2. How secure is the data managed by Hostune's systems?

Hostune ensures robust security with encrypted communications, role-based access control, audit trails, and compliance with healthcare regulations like NABH standards to protect sensitive patient information.

3. Can Hostune integrate with existing medical devices and systems?

Absolutely! Hostune supports bi-directional integration with laboratory equipment, imaging systems, and external services using HL7 and ASTM protocols for seamless interoperability.

4. What types of hospitals can benefit from Tune HIS?

Tune HIS is a comprehensive Hospital Management System suitable for a wide range of healthcare providers, from single doctor clinics to multi and super specialty hospitals, as well as medical colleges

5. What kind of training and support is provided for Tune HIS users?

Hostune offers quick and consistent chat-based support. Their intuitive user interfaces are also designed to reduce the time spent on devices, helping users to focus on patient care.
